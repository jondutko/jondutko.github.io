BIO

From Humble Beginnings

Jon was born at Sibley Hospital in Bethesda, MD. At the age of five he was given his first pair of glasses. His first video game system was a Nintendo 64, acquired at the age of eight. He attended elementary school, and then middle school, and then high school. He survived all three of these schools.

A Journey Quest

Jon arrived at Tufts University in the Autumn of 2010. Since then he has made many friends and has maintained membership in several clubs. He is a radio DJ once a week.

The Future?

Jon's future is unclear, as no man can peer into the Sands of Time. To attempt to extrapolate a life's path from such a premature and fixed state would not only be foolhardy but an affront to God and nature.
